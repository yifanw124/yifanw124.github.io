     + geom_point(aes(x='PTS', y='AST'), data=annotation_df, size=5, color='red')

                 nudge_x=10, nudge_y=5, ha='left', va='bottom')

import statsmodels.formula.api as smf

# Creating a linear regression model

results = model.fit()

print(results.summary())

The coefficient for AST is approximately 2.3989. The p-value associated with the ‚ÄòAST‚Äô coefficient is ~0, which is very low (< 5%). This indicates that the relationship between assists and points is statistically significant; we can reject the null hypothesis that there is no relationship between them. To sum up - there‚Äôs a statistically significant and positive relationship between the number of assists and the total points scored by NBA players in this dataset, with assists explaining a moderate portion of the variation in points.
